Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I register on the portal?

Click on the Join us button on the top-right, fill in your details, and create your student, alumni, or recruiter account.

2. I forgot my password. What should I do?

Visit the login page and click on the Forgot Password option. You will receive an email to reset your password.

3. How can I apply for jobs?

Once logged in as a student, go to the Job Listings section and apply directly through the portal.

4. How do companies contact students?

Recruiters can directly message shortlisted candidates through the internal portal messaging system.

5. Whom should I contact for technical issues?

You can contact our support team using the form below.
